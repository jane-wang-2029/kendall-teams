kTeams Program Structure

The 2-week program is structured around several key components:

  • Orientation & Ecosystem Overview: Introduction to Boston innovation ecosystem and program expectations
  • Market Positioning Workshops: Sessions focused on refining value proposition and market strategy
  • Pitch Development: Guided development and practice of investor presentations
  • Strategic Introductions: Curated meetings with investors, partners, and potential customers
  • Site Visits: Tours of relevant companies, labs, and institutions
  • Networking Events: Opportunities to connect with entrepreneurs, mentors, and ecosystem players
  • Individual Consultations: One-on-one sessions with mentors and advisors
  • Program Wrap-up: Final presentations and action plan development
