kTeams Program

kTeams Program

The kTeams program is an intensive 2-week experience designed to help international scale-ups understand, navigate, and engage with the US market. The program combines structured learning sessions, networking events, strategic introductions, and hands-on market exploration activities.

Throughout the program, teams work directly with experienced mentors and advisors to refine their market strategy, develop investor materials, and build relationships with key stakeholders in their target market.

Sample kTeams Program

A typical kTeams program schedule includes:

Week 1

  • Program orientation and ecosystem overview
  • Market positioning and value proposition workshops
  • Initial investor and partner introductions
  • Site visits to relevant companies and institutions
  • Networking events and community engagement

Week 2

  • Pitch development and refinement sessions
  • Strategic investor and customer meetings
  • Continued ecosystem exploration and networking
  • Individual mentor consultations
  • Final presentations and action plan development

Specific schedules are customized based on participant needs, industry focus, and program timing.
